About ShutterShare

Welcome to ShutterShare, this application was designed as part of a final year project for Queen's University Belfast. The overall objective of the system is to allow you, the user to fully immerse yourself in high sophisticated searching platform. It will allow you to carry out core searching functionality such as keyword searching and geospatial searching. Just simply input the data you wish to search for, or share your location and be captivated by all the attractions and sites in your city of choice!
